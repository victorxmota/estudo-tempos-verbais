Present Simple

Explanation

The Present Simple is used for habitual actions, general truths, and fixed arrangements.

  • Affirmative: Subject + base verb (+ s/es for he/she/it)
  • Negative: Subject + do/does not + base verb
  • Question: Do/Does + subject + base verb?

Examples

  • I work every day.
  • She goes to school.
  • Do they play football?
