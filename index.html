Capítulo 1: Present Simple

📘 Explicação

O Present Simple é usado para falar sobre hábitos, rotinas, verdades universais, sentimentos e fatos permanentes.

  • 🌞 Rotinas e hábitos: I wake up at 7 a.m.
  • 🧠 Verdades universais: The Earth orbits the Sun.
  • 💬 Sentimentos/opiniões: She likes chocolate.

🧠 Regras gramaticais

Forma afirmativa:

  • Sujeito + verbo (forma base) → I work, You eat
  • Para he/she/it: adicionar -s ou -es → He works, She watches

Forma negativa:

  • Sujeito + do/does + not + verbo (forma base)
  • I do not (don’t) play. / She does not (doesn’t) like coffee.

Forma interrogativa:

  • Do/Does + sujeito + verbo (forma base)?
  • Do you work? / Does he play football?

🔊 Frases com pronúncia britânica

  • She drinks tea every morning.
  • They work in London.
  • He doesn’t like pizza.

✍️ Exercício

Complete a frase com o verbo correto no Present Simple:

He _____ (to go) to the gym every day.
